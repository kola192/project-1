Who We Are

Paradise Jet is a flight support company established in 2016, catering to over 200 clients and thousands of fliers annually. With a head office at Istanbul (TURKEY) and network offices in Egypt and Sudan (for African countries).

Our Vision

Paradise Jet is dedicated to being the world’s leading provider of aviation services with the overarching objective to deliver exceptional, long-term, sustainable value for all our clients.

What We Do

We provide all your logistics needs at a very high standard with on time delivery using only the latest technical and communications tools in the market. Paradise Jet preforms flight planning for all types of aircrafts, arranges itineraries and provides our clients with alternative plans. We obtain all necessary clearances, ground handling, fueling, as well as logistics assistance for the crew and passengers. This is done in order to provide a cost efficient trip and ensure total safety for our customers. Paradise Jet liaises with the concerned airports and personnel to ensure aircraft ground support and maintenance availability from landing until departure. We work on coordinating all phases on flight planning and operations including current weather conditions, recent NOTAMs, airport restrictions and fuel availability.
